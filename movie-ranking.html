THE BEST MOVIES ACCORDING TO ME

My top three movies of all time

Tere Ishk Mein

Critics offer mixed reviews for this complex story of toxic love, with some praising lead performances by Dhanush and Kriti Sanon, while others found the narrative messy and problematic.

Zootopia 2

The sequel to the beloved animated film continues to captivate audiences with its clever storytelling and vibrant animation, earning praise for its social commentary and humor.

Inception

Christopher Nolan's mind-bending thriller is lauded for its innovative plot, stunning visuals, and thought-provoking themes, solidifying its place as a modern classic in the sci-fi genre.
